ANNOUNCING THE CLOUD9 FATES NA QUALIFIER, PRESENTED BY RED BULL | CLOUD9

  • by StreamBeasles
  • 2 Comments
  • 15 Likes

Cloud9 is thrilled to be kicking off 2021 by announcing the Cloud9 Fates NA Qualifier, presented by Red Bull! This Teamfight Tactics qualifier will run from January 29th through January 31st and will feature high stakes including a $5,000 prize pool, four slots up for grabs at Fates Qualifier 4, and qualifier points towards the TFT North American Regional Finals.

Participants will qualify via four different pools:

  • Four players will join us from Liquid Fates Qualifier
  • Two players will join us from the Giant Slayer Challenger Series
  • Eight players will join us from the Team Liquid Open Qualifier Bracket
  • Ten players will join us from Giant Slayer’s January 20th weekly ladder snapshot (correction from the previously stated January 27th snapshot)

Here are the qualified players as of January 28th:

We also want to encourage players to continue competing at their highest levels throughout the month as all the top players on the ladder, especially those in the top 25, will be considered as we fill out the participants, so stay on your toes and keep an eye on your inboxes for any communication from Cloud9!


“We loved being a part of the Nebula NA Qualifiers last year, and we’re thrilled to again be hosting such an amazing TFT competition. This is going to be an exciting showcase of some of the best TFT players out there and we can’t wait to share it with the community.” —Gaylen Malone, Cloud9 Sr. General Manager

“Cloud9 is always looking for ways to support the NA ecosystem and being able to put NA TFT talent in the spotlight like this is an opportunity we’re grateful to be a part of. We look forward to the competitors and the community turning out in full force and I think it’s going to be a fantastic event for everyone.” —Jack Etienne, Cloud9 CEO and Co-Founder

The action kicks off on January 29th at 4PM PST on the Cloud9 Twitch channel and we can’t wait to see you there! For more updates on this and all things Cloud9, make sure to follow us on Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and Discord!
